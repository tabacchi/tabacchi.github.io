Escape Room


By: Jason Bauer, Lauren Bullock, Derek Erway, Thomas Tabacchi and Parker Wilson

Summary

A VR mobile game made with the Unity Engine and plays on Google Cardboard. The player must explore multiple diffirent rooms in order to find the required materials to solve the puzzles in each room. Rooms range from western to space.

What I Worked On

  • Building the anime themed room
  • Creating props using Maya
  • Designing a puzzle
  • Creating a fully decorated room that looks accurate
