Dev Heck


By: Jason Bauer, Lauren Bullock, Derek Erway, Thomas Tabacchi and Parker Wilson

Summary

A bullet hell made with the Unity Engine where the player has to avoid bullets while killing the enemy and picking up the power ups. This game is a joke about the whole game developement process. The enemys are missing files and the player is Unitys default for a missing texture. Comes with a cornflower blue background.

What I Worked On

  • Spawing the enemies
  • Enemy movement
  • Random enemy mass spawn after killing one
  • Randomizing the shooting patterns
  • Increasing spawn rate the longer the game is
