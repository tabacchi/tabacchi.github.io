Bardo

By: Thomas Tabacchi, Jajuan Webster, Jared Miller, William Pennock and Sonya Sachdev

Summary

A team based competitive game where one team must scour the three floor map in order to find the ghosts hidden throughout the house. Each player has there own special ability to aid in finding the ghosts such as breaking through a wall. The other team is the ghosts who must avoid being caught by the hunters while also having to gain power to be able to possess the hunters. This is done by getting quests from the deck and haunting a certain room that is listed on the card.
