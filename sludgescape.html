Sludgescape

By: Thomas Tabacchi, Brandon Downing, Brandon Gulielmo and Will Pennock

Summary

Co-operative board game where everything in the game is out to kill you. In this post apocalyptic world you and five others must brave this wasteland in order to find the necessary equipment to kill the final boss that is threatening your already not so great way of life

What I worked On

  • Game balance
  • Creating over 150 loot cards
  • Play-testing
  • Prototyping
  • Budgeting
  • Writing rules
  • Pitching an idea
